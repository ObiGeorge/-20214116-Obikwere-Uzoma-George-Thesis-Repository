This document contains the table name description for the tables in the "Current Relevant Available Cancer Data for 2016 England.xls" 
multi-dataset. The tables in the multi-dataset were selected from the "cancerregistrations2016final.xlsx" file  which was provided by the 
"National Cancer Registration and Analysis Service within Public Health England; Office for National Statistics" via
https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/healthandsocialcare/conditionsanddiseases/bulletins/cancerregistrationstatisticsengland/2016

Table numbers and description:

Table 1: Registrations of newly diagnosed cases of cancer (third digit): site‚ sex and age‚ England, 2016

Table 2: Registrations of death from cancer (third digit): site‚ sex and age‚ England, 2016

Table 3: Registrations of newly diagnosed cases of cancer (third digit): site‚ sex and region of residence‚ England, 2016

Table 4: Registrations of newly diagnosed cases of cancer (fourth digit): site, sex and age, England, 2016

Table 5: Registrations of newly diagnosed cases of cancer (third digit): site and sex, England, 1995 to 2016

Table 6: Registrations of death from cancer (third digit): site and sex, England, 2001 to 2016
